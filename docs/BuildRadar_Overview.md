## What We Do

BuildRadar is a local intelligence service for London tradespeople.

Every week, London councils publish approved planning applications — homeowners who have just received authorisation for extensions, loft conversions, garden rooms, roof work, and full refurbishments. These homeowners haven't started searching for a tradesperson yet. They've just received permission and don't know where to start.

BuildRadar identifies these **verified opportunities** the moment they are authorised and delivers them directly to local tradespeople via **WhatsApp every Friday morning** — before the homeowner opens Google.

---

## The Problem We Solve

Local builders, roofers, landscapers, and other tradespeople spend money on Checkatrade, MyBuilder, and Rated People — platforms where they compete against 5–10 other tradespeople on price, after the homeowner has already decided to search.

BuildRadar inverts this. The tradesperson arrives **before** the homeowner has searched for anyone. The window is 7 days. The first builder through the door typically wins the quote visit.

---

## How It Works

**1. Intelligence**
Our system monitors approved planning applications across London boroughs — currently Wandsworth (SW4, SW11, SW12, SW17). Each week produces 40–50 verified development triggers.

**2. Data Drop**
Every Friday morning, verified opportunities are packaged and sent to registered tradespeople via WhatsApp. Each lead includes:
- Job type (e.g. rear extension — authorised)
- Area (e.g. SW11 — Northcote Road Area)
- Estimated job value
- Full address — released when the tradesperson claims the lead

**3. Claim or Skip**
The tradesperson replies **CLAIM** to receive the full address and planning reference, or **SKIP** to pass. The system handles this automatically — no app, no login, no dashboard.

**4. The Conversion Kit**
Every claiming tradesperson receives the BuildRadar Conversion Kit:
- Pre-written letter template (print and post same day)
- Door-knock script — the exact first 10 seconds at the door
- Objection handlers
- 48-hour WhatsApp follow-up message

## The Email Outreach System

Before builders sign up to receive weekly Data Drops, we reach them via a personalised cold email campaign.

Each email is:
- **Trade-specific** — a roofer receives copy about loft conversions and roof work, a landscaper receives copy about garden rooms and outbuildings
- **Lead-first** — opens with a specific verified opportunity in their area, not a product description
- **Obfuscated** — the sample lead shows the area (e.g. *SW12 — Balham Area*) and a redacted planning reference (*2026/●●●●●/FUL*) but not the full address. The full address is released only on claim — creating a reason to engage
- **Linked to action** — the email CTA goes to `buildradar.co.uk/claim`, a mobile-optimised page with the door-knock script and 3-touch conversion sequence

Current outreach pipeline: **11 verified trade businesses** across Wandsworth (builders, roofers, landscapers, window/door installers, architects and structural engineers). Director names sourced via Companies House. Websites verified before trade category assigned.

## The Landing Page — buildradar.co.uk

The landing page is built for conversion, not explanation.

**Key elements:**
- **Scarcity bar** — live spot counter (e.g. *Wandsworth: 2/3 spots taken — 1 left*)
- **Sample lead card** — a real WhatsApp-style lead with hover-to-reveal address and a *Show door-knock script* toggle
- **Conversion Kit CTA** — *"Get the Lead + Conversion Kit — free"* (not just leads — the tools to win the job)
- **FAQ** — leads with *"How do I actually get through the door?"* — the highest-anxiety question answered immediately with the first 10 seconds script
- **Territory Manager** — postcode check on signup; if the area is full, redirects to /waitlist
- **No pricing shown** during pilot — response first, pricing conversation once they've seen the product work

## The Conversion Kit — buildradar.co.uk/claim

A public, mobile-first page available immediately after claiming a lead. Contains:

- **The 3-Touch Sequence:** Letter (Day 1) → Door knock (Day 3) → WhatsApp follow-up (Day 7)
- **First 10 Seconds at the door:** *"Hi, sorry to disturb you — my name's [name], I'm a local builder. I dropped a letter through your door a few days ago about your planning approval. Did you happen to see it?"*
- **Objection handlers** for the 5 most common doorstep responses
- **Pre-filled WhatsApp follow-up message** — tap to send
